COD Zombies

Note: The Empty Earth does not have dated events, in their place events have been named and put in order.

Events

THE DISCOVERY OF THE EMPTY EARTH - From the MPD, Orginal Dimension Maxis finds a Dimension in which life does not exist. He creates a facility here titled Zero Base, and secures the base with a robotic version of his wife Sophia. He fills the base with artifacts and replicas collected from a multitude of different timelines. Among these is the Kronoirum, a book chonricling every event in every timeline, it is put under high security.

THE REDISCOVERY OF THE EMPTY EARTH - Having observed Monty for some time. D63 Maxis, from the House in Agartha, uses the Teleporter in the basement to study and explore other dimensions. Among them is the Empty Earth. However, without a soul, his attempts to manipulate The Empty Earth creates a reality too fragile to be sustained.

VICTIS ARRIVES AT ZERO BASE - After securing the artifact at Broken Arrow Victis teleport to Zero Base. Sophia activates security open realizing the four are intruders. Victis fight their way through to find a room with four pods, sitting in them their blood is drawn and collected. The facility is filled with life the Victis take refuge in a hanger at the base.

VICTIS AQUIRES THE KRONORIUM - Stuhlinger, still acting on Primis Richtofen's voice in his head, is assured that this final action is how the group will achieve it's salvation. The other three disagree and Stuhlinger goes alone to obtain the book, but after facing trouble his comrades save him and help him to obtain the book. While Victis teleport to a base under Alactraz in D63, Undead Richtofen collects their blood from the pods they laid in.

THE BLOOD AQUIRED - After talking the Victis in Alcatraz, Primis Richtofen teleports to Empty Earth to collect the blood vials from Undead Richtofen.
